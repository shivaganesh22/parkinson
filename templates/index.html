About Parkinson's Disease

What is Parkinson's?

Parkinson's disease is a progressive nervous system disorder that affects movement. It develops gradually, sometimes starting with a tremor in just one hand.

Early Symptoms

Early signs include tremor, slow movement, rigid muscles, and voice changes. Our AI analyzes vocal patterns to detect subtle abnormalities in speech.

Early Detection

Early diagnosis allows for better treatment planning. Regular monitoring helps track disease progression and enables timely medical interventions.
